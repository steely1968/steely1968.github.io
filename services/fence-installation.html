Why Choose Featheredge Fencing?

Featheredge fencing offers excellent privacy and strength. Each board overlaps the next, creating a strong barrier that stands up well to wind, rain, and time.

Concrete posts and gravel boards keep timber off the ground, which dramatically reduces rot and extends the life of your fence.
