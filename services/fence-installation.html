Fence Installation in Askern DN6

Home Repairs 4U provides professional fence installation in Askern DN6 and surrounding areas. We install strong, long-lasting fencing designed to give privacy, security, and a clean finish to your garden or property boundary.

Our most popular and reliable fencing system is featheredge fencing with concrete posts and gravel boards. This type of fencing is ideal for British weather and offers excellent durability compared to lightweight panel systems.

Our Main Fencing System

The typical type of fence we install is:

  • Featheredge boards for strength and privacy
  • Concrete posts for maximum stability
  • Concrete gravel boards to protect timber from rot

This system is built on site, not slotted in like cheap panels. Each featheredge board overlaps the next, creating a solid fence that can handle wind, rain, and long-term use.

Why Choose Featheredge Fencing?

Featheredge fencing is one of the strongest and most professional-looking fencing options available. Because it is built board by board, it can be adjusted to uneven ground, slopes, and awkward boundaries.

Concrete posts and gravel boards stop timber from sitting directly on soil, which greatly reduces rot and extends the life of your fence.

Fence Installation Process

Every fence installation follows a proper process:

  • Old fencing removed and disposed of
  • Post holes dug to correct depth
  • Concrete posts set and levelled
  • Concrete gravel boards installed
  • Featheredge boards fixed securely
  • Final checks for strength and alignment

We take pride in straight lines, solid fixing, and a neat finish.

Repair or Replace?

If your fence is leaning, broken, or rotten at the base, it is often better to replace it fully using concrete posts and gravel boards. This prevents the same problems coming back in a few years.

We also offer fence repairs where possible, but we will always give honest advice on whether repair or replacement is best value long-term.
