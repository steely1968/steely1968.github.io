Service scope — what we do

We deliver durable exterior finishes that protect and improve the look of properties across Askern, Doncaster and the DN6 area.

  • Exterior masonry & render painting
  • Render repair, crack filling & breathable coatings
  • Fascia, soffit, gutter and bargeboard painting (UPVC & timber)
  • Window frames, external doors and timber cladding
  • Metalwork: gates, railings and handrails (rust treatment & paint)
  • Fence, shed and outbuilding staining
  • Power washing, algae & moss removal prior to painting

Materials & guarantees

We use trusted brand paints and follow product datasheets for correct preparation and application. Typical workmanship guarantee: 2 years (subject to product warranties and normal wear).

Safety and access

Work at height is managed with secure ladders or short-term scaffolding as required. We comply with safe working-at-height guidance and carry public liability insurance.

Our process — clear & reliable

  1. Survey & free quote: Site visit, photos and a written quote with a clear scope and colour advice.
  2. Preparation: Pressure wash, remove loose paint, repair damaged areas, prime where needed.
  3. Painting: Apply recommended primer and two coats of high-quality exterior paint.
  4. Finish & tidy: Inspection, client walkthrough and full site clean-up.

Do you provide a written quote?

We monitor conditions and only paint in suitable weather; where needed we will reschedule for the best finish.
Do you use scaffold?
We use secure ladders for smaller jobs and short-term scaffolding for larger or high-access projects. Scaffolding is quoted separately if required.
