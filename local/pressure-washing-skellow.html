How We Work

Each project begins with a thorough assessment of your surfaces. We examine the type of material, its condition, and any delicate areas to ensure the cleaning method is perfectly matched. This avoids damage while ensuring maximum cleaning efficiency.

Heavily soiled or moss-covered surfaces are pre-treated when necessary to loosen grime. Then, our team applies precise pressure washing, focusing on corners, joints, and edges for an even, professional finish.

Optional follow-up treatments include block paving re-sanding, sealing, and anti-algae/fungicide applications. These enhance durability, reduce maintenance, and keep surfaces looking cleaner for longer.
