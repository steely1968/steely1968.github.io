Our Approach to Pressure Washing

Each project begins with a detailed inspection of the surface. We assess the type of material, its age, and areas requiring special care. This allows us to adjust water pressure, cleaning solutions, and techniques to achieve the best results.

Stubborn dirt, moss, and algae are treated before pressure washing begins. We clean edges, joints, and hard-to-reach areas thoroughly, leaving an even and consistent finish. Optional re-sanding and sealing treatments improve durability and help maintain a clean appearance longer.

Our Services

Driveway Cleaning

We remove dirt, oil, tyre marks, moss, and algae from block paving, tarmac, and concrete driveways. Re-sanding restores joint stability and prevents weeds.

Patio & Paving Cleaning

Patios are cleaned thoroughly without damaging joints or stone surfaces. Moss, weeds, and black spot are removed safely and effectively.

Decking Cleaning

Decking surfaces are gently cleaned to remove grime and algae, preserving the wood and preparing it for sealing if desired.

Walls, Steps & Pathways

Walls, steps, and pathways are cleaned to remove embedded dirt, moss, and algae. This improves safety and enhances the visual appeal of your property.

Maintenance Advice

Regular cleaning protects your surfaces from long-term damage. Shaded areas and high-traffic paths benefit from annual cleaning, while occasional re-sanding or sealing prolongs the lifespan of block paving, patios, and decking.
