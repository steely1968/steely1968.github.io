Why Looking After Your Gutters Matters

In Skellow, seasonal weather and nearby trees often cause leaves, moss, and debris to collect inside gutters. When water cannot drain properly, it spills over the edge and runs down exterior walls. Over time, this can lead to damp patches, internal staining, and deterioration of brickwork and timber.

Routine maintenance helps avoid these problems and keeps rainwater moving safely away from your home. It also reduces strain on gutter joints and fixings, helping the system last longer.
