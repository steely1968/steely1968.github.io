Frequently Asked Questions

What is pressure washing?

Pressure washing is a cleaning method that uses high-pressure water to remove dirt, grime, algae, mould, and stains from hard surfaces. It is commonly used on driveways, patios, walls, and outdoor areas to restore their appearance.

How is pressure washing different from power washing?

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water without heat, while power washing uses heated water. Power washing is better for grease and oil stains, whereas pressure washing is ideal for general dirt and organic growth.

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

Not all surfaces are suitable for high pressure. Delicate materials like roof tiles, render, and painted surfaces require soft washing or lower pressure to avoid damage.

How often should pressure washing be done?

Most residential properties benefit from pressure washing once a year. High-traffic or shaded areas may need cleaning more frequently to prevent algae and moss build-up.

Can pressure washing remove algae and moss?

Yes, pressure washing effectively removes surface algae and moss. For long-term results, treatments or soft washing solutions are often used to prevent quick regrowth.

Does pressure washing damage block paving?

If done incorrectly, it can remove jointing sand. Professional pressure washing includes re-sanding to maintain stability and appearance.

Is pressure washing environmentally friendly?

When done professionally, pressure washing can be eco-friendly by using water-efficient equipment and biodegradable cleaning products.

Can pressure washing increase property value?

Yes, clean exterior surfaces improve kerb appeal, making properties more attractive to buyers or tenants.

How long does pressure washing take?

The time depends on the size and condition of the area. Most driveways or patios can be cleaned within a few hours.

Is pressure washing suitable for commercial properties?

Yes, it is widely used for shop fronts, car parks, and walkways to maintain a clean and professional appearance.

Can I pressure wash my property myself?

DIY pressure washing is possible, but incorrect pressure or technique can cause damage. Hiring a professional ensures safe and effective results.

What are the benefits of hiring a professional pressure washing service?

Professionals use the correct equipment, pressure levels, and cleaning methods to deliver long-lasting results while protecting surfaces and saving time.
