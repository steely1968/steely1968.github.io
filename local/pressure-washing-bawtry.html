Frequently Asked Questions About Pressure Washing in Bawtry

What surfaces can be pressure washed?
Pressure washing is suitable for many exterior surfaces, including driveways, patios, decking, walkways, fences, walls, garage doors, gutters, and conservatory roofs. Each surface is cleaned using the correct pressure and technique.

Is pressure washing safe for all materials?
Yes. Professionals adjust both pressure and cleaning methods depending on whether the surface is block paving, concrete, tarmac, stone, timber, or render to prevent damage.

How often should exterior surfaces be pressure washed?
Most properties benefit from annual cleaning. High-traffic or shaded areas may require more frequent washing to control algae and moss growth and maintain safety.

Can pressure washing remove oil stains and tyre marks?
Yes. Professional driveway pressure washing removes oil stains, tyre marks, and ingrained dirt. Heavily stained areas may need additional treatments for best results.

Does pressure washing improve safety?
Absolutely. Removing algae, moss, and grime significantly reduces slippery surfaces, helping prevent slips and accidents

Is pressure washing environmentally friendly?
Yes. We use water efficiently and only apply biodegradable cleaning products when necessary, ensuring safety for plants, pets, and surrounding areas.

How long does pressure washing take?
Job duration depends on surface size and condition. Smaller areas may take a few hours, while larger properties or commercial sites may take a full day.

Can pressure washing restore older walls?
Yes. It removes dirt, algae, moss, and pollution stains from brick, stone, render, and cladding, helping prevent further deterioration.

Do I need to prepare my property beforehand?
Very little preparation is needed. We recommend moving vehicles, furniture, and lightweight items from the area. Additional guidance is provided for delicate surfaces if required.
