Our Professional Pressure Washing Services

✔ Driveway Pressure Washing

Block paving, concrete, resin-bound and tarmac driveways collect years of dirt, moss, tyre marks, algae and oil. Our professional cleaning process lifts deep-set grime and organic growth, restoring both appearance and safety.

✔ Patio & Paving Cleaning

Patios often accumulate moss, weeds, lichen and black spot due to moisture and limited sunlight. We restore natural colour while protecting the surface.

✔ Decking Cleaning

Wooden decking requires skilled handling. Our approach safely removes algae and dirt without splintering the timber.

✔ Walls, Fences & Brickwork

Exterior walls and fences gradually darken as pollutants embed into the surface. Pressure washing instantly refreshes the property exterior.

✔ Paths, Steps & Access Areas

Paths and steps become hazardous when algae builds up. Regular pressure washing reduces slip risk and improves safety.
