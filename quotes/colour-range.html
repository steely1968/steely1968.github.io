Original House

This image shows a typical exterior that has become tired, dirty and run down over time. This is how we would normally approach a house like this. First, we would start by cleaning and repairing the guttering and downpipes to ensure water is draining correctly. At the same time, we would restore the uPVC so it looks clean, bright and refreshed again. Next, the exterior would be treated with a fungicidal wash to kill off moss, algae and mould. In this case, sealing would not be required because the house has already been painted and the surface is still sound. After that, we would repair small damage such as old cable holes and cracks before finally painting and transforming the home.
