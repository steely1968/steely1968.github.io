Frequently Asked Questions

How long does metal fence and gate paint last?

When metal is properly cleaned, treated, primed, and painted, the finish can last for many years. Exposure to weather, sun, and moisture affects lifespan, but good preparation makes a huge difference.

Yes. Loose rust and flaking paint are removed first. The metal is then treated before painting so the new coating sticks properly and helps prevent rust returning.

We use quality metal paints designed for outdoor use. These are made to resist rust, fading, and peeling in British weather conditions.
