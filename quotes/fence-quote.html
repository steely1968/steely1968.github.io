Frequently Asked Questions

Is painting or staining better for wood?

Paint gives a solid colour and strong protection. Stain soaks into the wood and shows the grain. Both protect the timber when applied properly.

How long does fence paint or stain last?

Most finishes last several years depending on weather exposure and preparation. Proper cleaning and drying makes a big difference.

Do you clean fences before painting or staining?

Yes. Dirt, algae, and loose coatings are removed first so the new finish sticks properly.
