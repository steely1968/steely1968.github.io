Fence Details

Why Choose Featheredge Fencing

Featheredge fencing is one of the strongest fencing systems available. It allows for sloping ground, uneven levels and bespoke heights. Combined with concrete posts and gravel boards, it resists rot, moisture and storm damage.

It is ideal for gardens, driveways, side boundaries and commercial yards. It can be painted or stained and easily repaired if individual boards are damaged.
