Featheredge Fence Installation in Askern

We specialise in installing strong, long-lasting featheredge fencing using concrete posts and concrete gravel boards. Every fence is built to your required height and layout rather than forcing you into standard sizes.

What This Calculator Includes

This calculator gives you an instant estimated price based on your fence length, bespoke height, posts, panels, gravel boards, ground conditions, hedges, trees and gates. It calculates materials and labour so you can understand what goes into your price before booking.

If your fence runs over mixed ground such as soil and concrete, you can enter how many panels sit on each surface. Concrete ground requires extra work and is priced accordingly. Heavy rooted ground also requires more labour and is included in the calculation.

Gates, Access and Custom Layouts

You can add as many gates as you need. Gates can be single or double, built as part of the fence line or as a standalone garden entrance. If a gate is part of the fence, the calculator automatically deducts the fence materials and labour that are replaced by the gate.

Gate prices are calculated by width in metres, allowing you to design your access exactly how you want it rather than being forced into standard sizes.

Hedges, Trees and Site Clearance

If hedges need removing to install your fence, you can add the total length in metres and this will be priced automatically.

If trees are present, you can enter their height. Trees under 3 metres are priced directly in the calculator. Trees over 3 metres require specialist equipment and will be quoted as a separate tree felling service.

Why Choose Featheredge Fencing

Featheredge fencing is one of the strongest and most flexible fencing systems available. It allows for sloping ground, uneven levels and custom heights while remaining secure and long-lasting. Combined with concrete posts and gravel boards, it offers excellent resistance to rot, moisture and storm damage.

This type of fencing is ideal for gardens, driveways, side boundaries, commercial yards and rural properties. It can be painted or stained to match your property and easily repaired if individual boards are ever damaged.

Serving Askern and Surrounding Areas

We provide fence installation across Askern and all areas within a six mile radius. This includes surrounding villages, housing estates, rural properties and new developments. Our local knowledge allows us to price fairly, arrive quickly, and complete work efficiently.
