Why Choose Featheredge Fencing

Featheredge fencing is strong and flexible, allowing for sloping ground, uneven levels and custom heights while remaining secure and long-lasting. Combined with concrete posts and gravel boards, it offers excellent resistance to rot, moisture and storm damage.

This type of fencing is ideal for gardens, driveways, side boundaries, commercial yards and rural properties. It can be painted or stained to match your property and easily repaired if individual boards are ever damaged.
