FAQs: Patio Pressure Washing

1. What is patio pressure washing?

Patio pressure washing uses controlled high-pressure water to remove dirt, algae, moss, lichen, and stains from patio surfaces.

2. Is pressure washing safe for patios?

Yes. Pressure levels are carefully adjusted to suit patio slabs, paving stones, concrete, and natural stone surfaces.

Concrete slabs, block paving, sandstone, limestone, porcelain tiles, and natural stone patios can all be safely cleaned.

Preparation includes removing furniture, clearing debris, inspecting joints, and applying pre-treatment for algae or moss.
