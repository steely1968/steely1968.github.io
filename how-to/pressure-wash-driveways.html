FAQs: Pressure Washing Driveways and Outdoor Surfaces

1. What is pressure washing?

Pressure washing is a professional cleaning method that uses high-pressure water to remove dirt, moss, algae, oil stains, and grime from hard surfaces.

2. Is pressure washing safe for my driveway?

Yes. Pressure levels are professionally controlled to clean without damaging block paving, concrete, tarmac, or stone surfaces.

3. What driveway surfaces can be pressure washed?

Driveways made of block paving, concrete, tarmac, imprinted concrete, and natural stone can all be cleaned safely.

4. How do you prepare a driveway before pressure washing?

Preparation includes clearing debris, inspecting surface and joints, and applying pre-treatment where moss or algae is present.

5. Do you use chemicals during pressure washing?

Eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaners are used only when necessary to break down stubborn stains and growths.

6. How long does driveway pressure washing take?

Most jobs take between 1–3 hours depending on the size and condition of the driveway.

7. Can pressure washing remove oil stains?

Yes, specialist degreasers combined with pressure washing can significantly reduce or remove oil stains.

8. Do you pressure wash patios as well?

Yes. We also clean patios, walls, decking, and fencing along with driveways.

9. Will pressure washing remove weeds and moss?

Pressure washing removes visible weeds, moss, and algae, helping to slow regrowth and improve safety.

10. Do I need to be home during the service?

No, as long as we have access and water supply, the service can be completed without you present.

11. What happens after pressure washing a driveway?

The surface is rinsed thoroughly; block paving joints may be re-sanded for lasting results.

12. How often should a driveway be pressure washed?

Every 12–24 months depending on traffic, shade, and weather exposure.

13. Can pressure washing damage block paving?

No. Professional control prevents damage while achieving a deep clean.

14. Is pressure washing environmentally friendly?

Yes, we use water-efficient equipment and eco-friendly solutions.

15. Is pressure washing weather dependent?

Most weather conditions are fine, though heavy rain or freezing temperatures are avoided.

16. Can walls and fencing be pressure washed?

Yes, exterior walls, fencing, and other outdoor surfaces can be safely cleaned.

17. Do you offer commercial pressure washing?

Yes, including car parks, walkways, and commercial properties.

18. Is pressure washing noisy?

Some noise occurs, but disruption is minimal.

19. How much does driveway pressure washing cost?

Costs vary by size and condition. Free quotes are available.
